Privacy Policy for Muslim Assistant App by Ninety+

Last Updated: November 23, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to the Muslim Assistant app developed by Ninety+. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we handle any information and data related to your use of our app.

Information We Collect

The Muslim Assistant app does not collect or store any personal data from its users. However, we do access the user's precise and approximate location to provide location-specific features within the app.

Location Data

To offer location-based services such as prayer times and Qibla direction, we require access to your precise and approximate location. This data is processed directly within your device and is not stored by us.

Use of Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes third-party services to enhance user experience and app functionality:

  • Firebase: Used for app functionality and performance monitoring.
  • Google Analytics: Employed for anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance.
  • AdMob and Other Ad Services: Used for serving ads within the app. These services may collect data as per their own policies, independent of Ninety+.

Data Protection

We are committed to protecting user privacy and handle all data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Since we do not collect personal data, there is minimal risk of data breach concerning personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at muslimassistant@mail.ru.
